The 4 hours principle Manifesto...

The architecture of the site was inspired by simplicity. There is no ad-blocker or other fancy features that will force you to sit down for 4 hours and do work. We, humans, are too intelligent to the point that lying to ourselves is pretty simple.

I came across the 4 hours principle by reading the Book "The one thing" by Gary Keller. This principle just works when it comes to high performance.

First-off the 4 hours principle is all about working 4 hours straight away from all distractions (all distractions you can think of...), the only excuse is going to the bathroom if you definitely have to. I consider the block as perfect because it is long enough to get serious work done and short enough to respond to your bodies need.

This principle has shaped my mindset about the relationship between time and productivity. Now I view any days in 4 hours blocks and it even helps me better structure my days. During the week-end I might apply 2 (4hours) blocks, when it gets hectic, I jump towards 4 blocks which gives you a total 16hrs of full concentration.


Try this and you will wonder why you didn't know of this principle earlier. Enjoy and be proud of your high performance.
